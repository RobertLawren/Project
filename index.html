To be eligible for a permit you will need to be a Rotherham resident. We can only grant van and pickup permits when this is the householders own vehicle and they have no alternative vehicle which can access the Household Waste Recycling Centre without a permit.

Unfortunately we are only able to accept 5 x 25kg bags per 6 months for DIY waste such as rubble, wood and plasterboard.

The Council's bulky waste service is operating as normal. We can help you with the removal and disposal of larger household items such as furniture, electrical appliances and DIY waste, such as bathroom suites, window frames, doors from residential properties.
